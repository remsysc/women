Herstory

Helena Z. Benitez

Renowned as the first Filipino woman to head the UN CSW, Helena Z. Benitez was instrumental in advancing women's rights, including the adoption of the Declaration on the Elimination of Discrimination Against Women.

Leticia Ramos-Shahani

A trailblazing diplomat and legislator, she urged preparations for the Philippines’ participation in global women's movements and led significant advancements for women's rights.

National Federation of Women’s Clubs

The organization spearheaded campaigns for suffrage in the 1920s, culminating in the recognition of women’s right to vote during the 1937 plebiscite.
